Located at the World's Oldest Airport, the museum features early era aircaft and exhibits on the history & science of flight.

Bike Share

Zagster BikeShare has partnered with U of MD and The City of College Park to provide bikeshare stations throughout the area. There is a station right outside The Hotel. A great way get around!

Lake Artemesia

This public park contains a 38-acre lake and is part of the extensive hiker-biker trails in the area. A wonderful location to spot birds, beavers (early morning!), deer, fox and other critters. It is about a 30 minute walk from The Hotel along a paved, tree-lined hiker-biker trail.

Learn More...
Fishnet Restaurant

Fishnet is a local restaurant featuring fresh seafood, salads, beer & wine. It is a 20 minute walk from The Hotel.

Learn More...
National Archives

The National Archives maintains a state-of-the-art archival facility in College Park. One-hour tours are available or do your own archival research!
