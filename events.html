1. Events

 IVOA Meeting

The meeting of the International Virtual Observatory Alliance (IVOA) will take place in The Hotel on November 8-10, 2018 in College Park, MD. The same preferred room rate has been reserved as for the ADASS conference. Please note that registration for IVOA is separate from registration for ADASS. The IVOA maintains a IVOA Interoperability meeting wiki page where you can find up to date information.

 Hackathon

We will host a Hackathon on Time Domain Astronomy on November 10-11, 2018 in the Physical Sciences Complex at the University of Maryland. This is a community event, drawing people from a wide range of backgrounds, high school, college and professionals, who can form teams or work on their own. With help from our domain experts, participants can create their own view of Time Domain Astronomy. We will also award 3 cash prizes donated by special sponsors for this event. The winner will be invited to present their hack at the start of the Time Domain Astronomy session on Wednesday morning. Details about location and registration information are available here.

 ADASS Tutorials

ADASS Tutorials are educational sessions conducted as a short class on a subject of general interest. The main goal of a Tutorial is to teach skills to participants. Attendance is free for registrants of the conference and $50 for non-registrants if space available (to be paid on-site). Tutorials take place on the afternoon of Sunday, November 11. It is recommended to bring a laptop, as most tutorials will post instructions well before ADASS on how to prepare your laptop to run through the tutorials.

 Welcome Reception

 Conference Banquet

The conference banquet will be held Tuesday, November 13, 2018, on the roof of The Hotel. More details will be posted when available.
