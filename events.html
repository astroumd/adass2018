1. Events

 IVOA Meeting

The meeting of the International Virtual Observatory Alliance (IVOA) will take place November 8-10, 2018 in College Park, MD. Details about location and registration information will be posted when available.

 Hackathon

We will host a Hackathon on Time Domain Astronomy on November 10-11, 2018. Details about location and registration information will be posted when available.

 ADASS Tutorials

ADASS Tutorials are educational sessions conducted as a short class on a subject of general interest. The main goal of a Tutorial is to teach skills to participants. Attendance is free for registrants of the conference and $50 for non-registrants. Tutorials take place on the afternoon of Sunday, November 11.

 Welcome Reception

A welcome cocktail reception will occur Sunday, November 11, 2018 from 6:00pm to 9:00pm in The Hotel. More details will be posted when available.

 Conference Banquet

The conference banquet will be held Tuesday, November 13, 2018, on the roof of The Hotel. More details will be posted when available.
