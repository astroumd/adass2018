Rosly Renil







Theme: Data Science: Workflows Hardware Software Humanware
Title: MeerKAT: Operational Workflow and Data Analysis

MeerKAT, the next generation radio telescope is already operational for commissioning and analysing large datasets for science community. This poster explains the operational processes in place for engineers, scientists, commissioners, etc to analyse the data-sets for further data mining that improves various processes. It is also worth to note the software processes and hardware involved, different analysis tools used for telescopic operations in producing good science data.
