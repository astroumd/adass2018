P5.13: Mink, Jessica
Jessica Mink (Smithsonian Astrophysical Observatory)







Theme: Science Platforms: Tools for Data Discovery and Analysis from Different Angles
Title: Finding Your Place in the Cosmos with WCSTools

The WCSTools package has been updated to include access to arbitrary-length keywords in FITS headers and the UCAC5 and GAIA catalogs. Examples of the use of those and older little -known features will be presented.
