Title: JWST Association Generation: Piecing It All Together

The basic unit of JWST data products is an exposure for each science instrument detector. Relationships between multiple exposures are captured in an association. An association is a means of identifying a set of exposures that belong together and may be dependent upon one another. JWST associations are created by the Association Generator. Using a set of association rules, the Generator filters through an Association Pool, basically a list of exposures and related meta-data, to produce Associations. These associations are then used as input to the appropriate calibration pipelines.
