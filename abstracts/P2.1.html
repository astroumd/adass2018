Mark Allen (CNRS, Observatoire astronomique de Strasbourg, UMR 7550, F-6700 Strasbourg)






Toward common solutions for data access, discovery and interoperability

The ASTERICS project has supported a program of work to make enablethe data from large astronomy infrastructures (ESFRI) and pathfinder projects to become available for discovery and usage by the whole astronomical community, interoperable in the Virtual Observatory framework, and accessible with a set of common tools. We highlight the successes and lessons learned.
